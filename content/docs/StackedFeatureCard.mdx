# `StackedFeatureCard`

A vertically-aligned card for showcasing a key feature with an icon, title, and description—ideal for rows or section-based layouts.

<Divider />

## ✨ Overview

`StackedFeatureCard` is ideal for:

- Marketing or feature sections with vertically-stacked cards
- Highlighting benefits with icons or custom visuals
- Working inside grid, flexbox, or sectioned layouts
- Dark or light themes with minimal overrides
