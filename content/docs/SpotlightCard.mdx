# `SpotlightCard`

A visually striking card layout with image, headline, and optional call-to-action content.  
Perfect for marketing rows, team features, or app showcases.

<Divider />

## âœ¨ Overview

`SpotlightCard` is designed to:

- Pair a visual (image/component) with a short blurb
- Anchor a section with a compelling CTA or highlight
- Be themeable with light or dark content
- Support alignment flexibility
