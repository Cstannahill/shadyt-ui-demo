# `PricingCard`

A component for showcasing subscription tiers, feature sets, or payment plans in a clean and comparative layout.

<Divider />

## ‚ú® Overview

`PricingCard` is perfect for:

- Highlighting pricing tiers with a clear call to action
- Presenting included features, value props, and plan badges
- Supporting toggleable billing cycles (e.g., monthly/yearly)
- Matching modern SaaS pricing grids
